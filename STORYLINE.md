## CHAPTERS

High-Level Narrative Arc (5 Chapters)
The game is structure into a 5 chapter arc. The structure uses the existing levels as the first two chapters and introduces three new, progressively more complex levels that reveal the core plot.

### Chapter 1: The Anomaly (Top-Down Maze Shooter)

Story: You investigate a strange energy signature that leads you to the Vault's entrance.
Gameplay: Navigate maze, collect rings, fight basic enemies. Introduction to drones, energy rings, and threat detection.

### Chapter 2: The Guardian (Top-Down Boss Fight)

Story: Confront an automated Vault defense system: the MazeGuardian.
Gameplay: Boss fight inside the maze. First major test of drone combat and movement.

### Chapter 3: The Corrupted Sector (Top-Down Puzzle Maze)

Story: Explore a decaying sector of the Vault and learn it is a parasitic machine.
Gameplay: Drone-based puzzles, environmental hazards, corrupted logs hidden behind ability-locked areas.

### Chapter 4: The Harvest Chamber (Vertical Scrolling Shmup)

Story: You're descending into a cavernous intake chamber filled with MH370 debris. A vertical shmup mimics the feeling of diving deeper into the Vault's guts.
Structure:

- Scrolling downward into wreckage, Vault debris, and harvested tech
- Obstacles: data fragments, rotating vault parts, falling debris
- Enemies: harvested drones, malfunctioning security
- Boss: corrupted Observer Core (half tech, half airplane AI)
- Goal: Retrieve the Quantum Circuitry payload at the bottom
